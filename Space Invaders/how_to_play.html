Space Invaders

How To Play

Description:

You are in control of a laser cannon which moves horizontally across the bottom of the screen and fires at enemy spaceships overhead, which are also firing their own projectiles back at you.

Each time your projectile hits a spacehip, your number of points goes up by 10.

Kill all the enemies to win!

Controls:

Arrow buttons: 'left arrow' button to move to the left and 'right arrow' button to move to the right - located at the bottom right of your keyboard.
